{%- comment -%} Normalize publication/talk dates. Accepts: YYYY-MM-DD -> exact day YYYY-MM -> first day of month for sorting YYYY -> Jan 1 for sorting Exposes: entry_date_iso entry_date_rank entry_date_display entry_date_precision {%- endcomment -%} {%- assign entry_date_value = include.date | default: include.year | append: '' | strip -%} {%- assign entry_date_parts = entry_date_value | split: '-' -%} {%- assign entry_date_precision = 'day' -%} {%- assign entry_date_iso = entry_date_value -%} {%- if entry_date_parts.size == 1 -%} {%- assign entry_date_precision = 'year' -%} {%- assign entry_date_iso = entry_date_parts[0] | append: '-01-01' -%} {%- elsif entry_date_parts.size == 2 -%} {%- assign entry_date_precision = 'month' -%} {%- assign entry_date_iso = entry_date_value | append: '-01' -%} {%- endif -%} {%- assign entry_date_rank = entry_date_iso | date: '%Y%m%d%H%M%S' -%} {%- if entry_date_precision == 'year' -%} {%- assign entry_date_display = entry_date_parts[0] -%} {%- else -%} {%- assign entry_date_display = entry_date_iso | date: '%b %Y' -%} {%- endif -%}
